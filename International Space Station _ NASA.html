BEAM Leak Checks While New Crew Preps for Launch

2 days ago
The weeks final set of CubeSats were deployed Wednesday night as the new BEAM goes through a series of leak checks before next weeks entry. Back inside the orbital lab, the six-member Expedition 47 crew conducted advanced space research sponsored by private and public institutions. A final pair of CubeSats was deployed outside the Kibo lab module Wednesday wrapping up the weeks deployment activities. Since Monday, a total of 16 Dove satellites were released into orbit from a small satellite deployer attached to Kibo. The CubeSats will observe the Earths environment helping disaster relief efforts and improving agricultural yields.
